Who I Am

Securing the Cloud, One Layer at a Time

I'm Md. Tawfik Imroz, a Cloud Security Engineer passionate about building and defending modern cloud infrastructure. My work sits at the intersection of security engineering, cloud architecture, and risk management — making organizations resilient against an ever-evolving threat landscape.

With hands-on experience across AWS, Azure, and GCP, I specialize in designing Zero Trust architectures, implementing DevSecOps practices, and operationalizing security programs that scale with growing organizations. I don't just identify problems — I build systems that prevent them.

My approach is rooted in the belief that security should enable the business, not hinder it. I work closely with development, operations, and leadership teams to integrate security seamlessly into every layer of the stack — from IAM policies to CI/CD pipelines to compliance frameworks.
